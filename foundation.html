our foundation

petabyte esports G2G is a selfless and innovation project targeting the underserved communities in nigeria. the idea aims to address people living in low-income urban area. offering a unique solution that can drive positive changes and improve quality life.

Petabyte Esports G2G refers to an organized competitive video gaming that involves youths in underserved communities learning and competing in various games. The industry has seen significant growth and has captured the attention of millions of teenagers worldwide. It therefore offers a unique platform for social interaction, skill development, and potential career opportunities for those in such under privileged environments

benefits

  • Social Engagement: Esports provides teenagers with opportunities to connect and collaborate with peers who share similar interests, fostering a sense of community and belonging.
  • Skills Development: Participating in Esports can enhance various skills, such as strategic thinking, problem-solving, teamwork, communication, and hand-eye coordination.
  • Educational Potential: Esports can be utilized as an educational tool, promoting cognitive abilities, critical thinking, and creativity.
