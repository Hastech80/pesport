Why Petabyte Esports Academy

The Academy is building the future of video gaming and Esports super brain where in the future we can leverage blockchain technology and artificial intelligence to build games and analyse predictions. We want to contribute to the growth, development and recognition of the gaming industry and also to have the opportunity to create our own local games using characters peculiar to us in our community.



Programs of the Academy are divided into two parts.

In the academy, we teach students how to code and develop games that can teach African History while leveraging blockchain technology. It will offer courses like Multimedia, Video Game Programming and Development, Computer Animation, Interactive Media Design and Game Art, Software Engineering, Sound Production, Graphic design and so on. We will provide training for digital enthusiasts who wish to get a head start on showcasing their talents and skills, and improving their overall presence in the digital world.



We will help develop students' visual storytelling skills and immerse them in hands-on video creation, learning the fundamentals of composition, shooting, writing, and editing in order to produce shareable quality content through training on the following:

  1. Training on techniques and tools for shooting and editing videos (mobile and computer)

  2. Learning controlled photography, and the general use of photography tools like cameras and smartphones

  3. Creating stories, storyboarding, and writing using light and audio effectively to better the quality of content originality, and how to connect with audiences

The AcademyÂ will welcome applicants from all backgrounds who have an interest in gaming and will be given a certification to that effect. Additionally, beneficiaries from the Petabyte G2G will receive free admissions and all educational expenses taken care of courtesy of the Foundation.


The other aspect of the academy focuses on empowering talent to become globally recognized professional Esports athletes and content creators by enhancing their skills. Those who apply to our academy will learn about game strategy, practise the games from a competitive angle and how to excel at international tournaments. We will provide training for gaming enthusiasts who wish to become professional Esports athletes with the potential to be recruited by top organizations. We will guide the enrolled gamers on a variety of gaming titles and share their experience through:

  1. Team building & synergy

  2. Game strategy & tactics

  3. Extensive training to improve gameplay and reflexes.

  4. Physical training to enhance endurance and physical abilities.

  5. Workshops on health and wellness to lead a healthy and sustainable lifestyle
